It's your skills that matter

At vmerg we focus on skill set

#skillsmatter #joinourteam



Out there in the job market, what employers look at is the right skills that fit in the job role.

Of course, college degrees are essential but the focus of individuals must be to sharpen their skills.
Precisely speaking all the skills cannot be measured with formal schooling and attaining degrees.
An employer looks for someone
  • who has mastered the art and
  • sharpened their skills
  • has basic values of dedication, honesty and punctuality
These values are equally essential along with the skill set.

What our youth must focus on is the ability and opportunities to enhance their skills over theoretical knowledge and degrees.
A chai wala is successful because he is skilled at the art of making chai and what keeps him on path is his level of commitment and integrity to keep his business going round the year.
The focus should be more on
  • sharpening communicative skills
  • inculcating right values and
  • learning the art to the highest degree of perfection
At vmerg, we have no bars on educational qualifications. We hire the people who are skilled, who align with our vision and have the fire within them to succeed and make a difference.

Skilled employees are the core of our firm and on them relies the growth of our firm.
